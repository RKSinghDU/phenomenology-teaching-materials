# Teaching Activities

12 hands-on activities for a PhD seminar in phenomenological psychology. Each includes format, timing, step-by-step instructions, materials, and debrief guidance.

| # | Activity | Format | Topic | Time |

## Suggested Sequencing

**Single day (3 hours):** Activities 02 → 01 → 03 → 07

**Three sessions:**
- Session 1: Activities 01, 02, 03, 04 (foundations)
- Session 2: Activities 07, 08, 09, 10 (method)
- Session 3: Activities 05, 06, 11, 12 (trauma & ethics)
